Weekly Assignment #2

WallStreetBets and GameStop

The week of 25 January 2021 was marked by attention to the extremely unusual trading activity involving the retailer GameStop. The company's stock saw huge fluctuations in prices and enormous volume in response to an online collective action campaign led by the sub-reddit /r/WallStreetBets to buy the stock and cause a "short squeeze" that inflicted billions of dollars of losses for some Wall Street hedge funds.
